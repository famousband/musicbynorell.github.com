Norell's single, "Champion Mindset", has received radio airplay and press coverage in the USA, Canada, Mexico, Italy, Spain, Portugal and more!

To Be Frank is a four-track EP that is the 2.5 album release by The Band Famous. Nearly the entire project was completed solely by co-founder and lead singer-songwriter, Norell, with the exception of the final track, "On Top", which was co-founder and former TBF member Zander's contribution to the project. Norell contributed vocals, recording and mixing to Zander's song.

Norell's single "Mermaid Energy" was featured and reviewed and even on the radio in Brazil on Heatwave!

Norell's single "Golden Rule" opens the EP, following with "Space Jam", featuring psychedelic rock vibes!

Last Words is the debut album by The Band Famous, once an app-exclusive album for iOS and Android, it was re-mastered and re-released on the album's 10-year anniversary.

The debut album is now available everywhere! It was discussed on Minnesota Public Radio and 89.3 FM The Current in the Twin Cities. "Escape", track #3 from the album, was featured in international artist Olek's Virtual Reality exhibition in NYC!
